Are you passionate about creating beautiful, functional living spaces? At Carloman University, we offer a range of programs designed to help students turn their love for home design into a rewarding career. Our Bachelor of Science in Interior Design program provides students with a comprehensive education in all aspects of interior design, from color theory and furniture design to space planning and lighting. Through engaging coursework and hands-on projects, students develop the critical thinking and problem-solving skills needed to create spaces that meet the needs of clients while also reflecting their personal style. For those interested in sustainable design, our Environmental Design program teaches students how to create homes and buildings that are eco-friendly and energy-efficient. Students learn about innovative materials and technologies that can reduce a building's carbon footprint and minimize its impact on the environment. With an emphasis on sustainability, this program prepares students to be leaders in the field of green design and to create buildings that are both beautiful and environmentally responsible. At Carloman University, we believe that technology plays a crucial role in modern home design. That's why we provide students with access to state-of-the-art 3D modeling software and other advanced tools to bring their designs to life. Through our Technology and Architecture program, students learn how to use cutting-edge software and hardware to create realistic models of homes and buildings, providing them with a competitive advantage in the job market. Whether you're interested in traditional or contemporary design, residential or commercial spaces, Carloman University has a program that will help you achieve your career goals in home design. With expert faculty, hands-on learning opportunities, and a commitment to innovation, Carloman University is the perfect place to turn your passion for home design into a successful and fulfilling career.
